Virtual Tour

Radisson Blu Sobieski Hotel

The Radisson Blu Sobieski Hotel is a conveniently located hotel that gets high ratings on Google. Some features of interest include free Wi-Fi and complimentary breakfast.

Warsaw Chopin Airport

The Warsaw Chopin Airport is the first place you'll see when you arrive in Poland. In addition to serving as a means of transportation for many people, this airport has also been involved in environmental protection programs.

STIXX Bar & Grill

STIXX Bar & Grill is a restaurant located just down the street from the Radisson Blu Sobieski Hotel. Their menu features many dishes inspired by countries around the world.

Restauracja Przy Zamku

Restauracja Przy Zamku is a restaurant located in Warsaw Old Town. Its atmosphere is primarily based on traditional polish traditions and culture, complete with a view of the Royal Castle.

Restauracja Polska Różana

Restauracja Polska Różana is a unique restaurant with an extremely light atmosphere. It features an in-restaurant garden accompanied by flowing piano music and the sounds of flowing water and birds.

Balbinka Kebab

Balbinka Kebab is is small, but memorable restaurant. It specializes in serving Polish style kebabs that are quite popular with those who live in the area.
