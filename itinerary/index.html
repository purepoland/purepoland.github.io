Itinerary

Day 1 (Saturday, June 23):

Between 11:25 AM and 12:00 PM - Arrive in Warsaw
12:00 PM to 12:30 PM - Ride the metro from Lotnisko Chopina-Przyloty 02 to Pl.Zawiszy 03 and walk to the Radisson Blu Sobieski Hotel.
12:30 PM to 1:00 PM - Check into hotel and unpack luggage.
1:00 PM to 2:00 PM - Eat lunch at the Trylogia resteraunt in the hotel.
2:00 PM to 2:30 PM - Walk to Rondo Daszyńskiego and take the metro to Centrum Nauki Kopernik. Walk to the Copernicus Science Center.
2:30 PM to 4:30 PM - Spend some time at the Copernicus Science Center.
4:30 PM to 5:00 PM - Walk to Centrum Nauki Kopernik and ride the metro to Rondo Daszyńskiego. Walk to the Warsaw Uprising Museum.
5:00 PM to 6:30 PM - Spend some time at the Warsaw Uprising Museum.
6:30 PM to 6:45 PM - Walk to STIXX Bar and Grill.
6:45 PM to 8:00 PM - Eat supper at STIXX Bar and Grill.
8:00 PM to __(fill in the blank)__- Enjoy the rest of your evening, take a walk in City Park, etc. Return to the hotel whenever you're ready.

Day 2 (Sunday, June 24):

6:15 AM to 7:00 AM - Wake up and prepare for breakfast.
7:00 AM to 8:00 AM - Eat breakfast in hotel lobby.
7:45 AM to 7:50 AM - Walk to metro station Pl.Zawiszy 03.
7:50 AM to 8:30 AM - Ride the metro to Emilii Plater 02 and then to Stare Miasto 01. Form there, walk to Warsaw Old Town.
8:30 AM to 10:00 AM - Spend some time at various shops, and explore Warsaw Old Town.
10:00 AM to 12:00 PM - Peruse the Royal Castle public museum within Old Town.
12:00 PM to 1:30 PM - Eat lunch at Restauracja Przy Zamku.
1:30 PM to 5:30 PM - Private Warsaw City Tour in a Communist Fire Van
5:30 PM to 7:00 PM - Eat supper at Der Elefant.
7:00 PM to 8:00 PM - Take a scenic walk through State Park.
8:00 PM to __(fill in the blank)__- Enjoy the rest of your evening! The nearest metro station is Pl.Bankowy 01 for when you need to return to the hotel.

Day 3 (Monday, June 25):

6:15 AM to 6:30 AM - Wake up and prepare for breakfast.
6:30 AM to 7:00 AM - Eat breakfast in hotel lobby.
7:00 AM to 5:00 PM - Krakow and Auschwitz Tour (Your tour guide will provide transportation.)
5:00 PM to 6:30 PM - Eat supper at Restauracja Polska Różana.
6:30 PM to 8:00 PM - Walk along the Vistula River.
8:00 PM to __(fill in the blank)__- Enjoy the rest of your evening! (You can get back to the hotel from Dworkowa 02 depending on how far you walked.)

Day 4 (Tuesday, June 26):

6:15 AM to 7:00 AM - Wake up and prepare for breakfast.
7:00 AM to 8:00 AM - Eat breakfast in hotel lobby.
8:00 AM to 8:10 AM - Ride the metro from Pl.Zawiszy 03 to Dw.Centralny 14.
8:10 AM to 10:00 AM - Spend some time at Złote Tarasy Shopping Mall.
10:00 AM to 10:30 AM - Take the metro from Dw.Centralny 25 to Pl.Na Rozdrożu 05, and walk to the University of Warsaw Botanic Garden.
10:30 AM to 12:30 PM - Spend some time at the Universtiy of Warsaw Botanic Garden.
12:30 PM to 1:00 PM - Travel back to the hotel to meet your food tour guide.
1:00 PM to 5:00 PM - Warsaw Food Tour
5:00 PM to 7:00 PM - Return to the hotel and take the segway tour.
7:00 PM to 8:00 PM - Eat supper at Balbinka Kebab Shop.
8:00 PM to __(fill in the blank)__- Enjoy the rest of your evening!
